# Freckle2Redmine

Import your time entries from Freckle to Redmine or ChillyProject.

You'll have to hack it to suit your needs.

## Usage

redmine2freckle yesterday

Will display yesterdays' time entries and their mapping to redmine
project, activity, issue.

For instance:

3 hours of "Dev, #123" in project "Awesome Client" will log 3 hours against the issue 123 under the category Dev.

Happy hacking!
